Howdy, I'm Amrita!

I grew up in Bangalore surrounded by gadgets, craft supplies and endless curiosity, so it's no surprise I landed somewhere between tech and design.

Engineering came first, then a very bold cold email landed me at IDC, IIT Bombay, where I discovered I actually love HCI. That took me to NID and then Samsung Research, where I explore how AI can shape visual experiences.

My journey runs on curiosity, hibiscus tea and far too many all nighters. I like diving into the unknown, asking too many questions and turning chaos into clarity.

When I'm not designing, I'm clicking photos, travelling or eating something great.

I'm still learning and growing and I'm really glad you're here.
